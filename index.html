About Aura Rhymes

Aura Rhymes is a fun and colorful kids channel where little explorers learn Rhymes, Shapes, Colours, and much more in a joyful and easy way. 🌱🐮🐔 With cute characters Aura Rhymes turn everyday learning into a delightful experience.


Each video helps children build curiosity, confidence, and imagination while enjoying engaging rhymes and stories. We create child-friendly, positive, and educational content that parents can trust. Every video is made with care to ensure a safe and enriching experience for young minds.


At Aura Rhymes the videos are made through colorful animations and plesant music. Designed for toddlers and preschoolers, our videos make learning simple, engaging, and full of smiles.

Our Story

We are a creative, family-driven team dedicated to building high-quality, safe, and meaningful content for children. Our team includes kids, young creators, mothers, and parents, allowing us to truly understand the needs, interests, and learning patterns of young minds.


Based on these insights, we thoughtfully design our videos to make learning engaging, simple, and enjoyable. Our mission is to combine fun with early learning in a way that nurtures curiosity and confidence in children.


At Aura Rhymes, we believe that children learn best through play, music, and imagination. That’s why our content focuses on creating a joyful environment where kids can explore, discover, and grow at their own pace. Every video is crafted with care to ensure it is age-appropriate, positive, and enriching.


Aura Rhymes is owned by AS Entertainment, a creative studio with a passion for storytelling and animation, AS Entertainment aims to create visually engaging and meaningful content that supports early childhood development.
